Welcoming Remarks

Antecedents instalments of Malaysian Students Technology Conference (MSTC) have been breakthrough by their own rights; the first being the pioneer and the second being the innovator. MSTC has and always will be a platform to congregate motivated students who are passionate about current and future technology and its impact on society. In the third instalment this year, we are shaping the conference to be interactive and impactful; coherent to our desire to be one of the most transformative and distruptive events happening in our world nowadays. As the Fourth Industrial Revolution is changing the world we know today, breakthroughs are being forged right in the very heart of the revolution. We are hoping to demonstrate the breakthroughs in technology that mankind has achieved thus far through the discovery of something by exploration and its significance. With shared experience from experts in numerous fields such as big data analytics, cybersecurity as well as many more up and coming fields, we are confident that MSTC is the platform to achieve this aspiration.

Meor Muhammad Hakeem
Executive Coordinator of MSTC 2018
University of Alberta
