Below is a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).

  1. What is MSTC all about and what can I gain from the event?
    MSTC is a conference that provides a platform for participants to engage and obtain insight upon challenges in the STEM industry, by its’ members from various backgrounds within the field. We believe that the exchange in ideas in between participants is the opportunity for everyone to gain understanding regarding modern technologies implemented within our societies and in hopes with that, we will be able to effectively improve the quality of life for others.

  2. Where will MSTC be held?
    This year’s MSTC venue will be announced soon. Stay tune!

  3. Who is/are the host(s) for MSTC?
    MSTC is the trademark event for YME, Young Malaysian Engineers. YME, which is a Malaysian students-run society based in the United Kingdom, serves to empower young, inexperienced engineers to develop within the STEM industry by establishing opportunities for the engagement between engineers and engineers-to-be.

  4. If the event is mainly focused towards engineers and engineers-to-be, is it also open to other Malaysian students/graduates from any other careers?
    We strongly encourage all Malaysian students studying within colleges and universities from all around the world to participate within the event, as it is open for all members within the Malaysian society to learn about the interesting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) topics and discover various career choices based on what was gained within the event itself!
